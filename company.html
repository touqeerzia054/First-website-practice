Getting Started With Your New Website

A small business’s online presence consists of three parts: a domain name, a website, and hosting services.

To receive a quote for a new website, or maintaining an existing site, complete our contact form. You can also call (618) 391-7070 to provide details about your business’s website needs.

Web Development quotes do not include domain name registration and web hosting services. See the tabs on the right of the screen for more information about these services.

Give us a call and let us know about your business, your business goals, and the objectives that you want your website to achieve. We’ll provide a quote based on your individual needs.

Customers have come to expect a business to possess a domain name that ends in .com. Accordingly, we recommend a .com that strongly describes your specific business or services industry. For example, we use CompanyWebsites.Com.

We advise that if you expect to frequently speak your domain name over the phone or advertise on the radio, choose a domain that is easy to say and spell aloud.

Are you a non-profit or similar organization? Consider registering the trusted .org extension. Above all, choose a domain name that is memorable and easy to spell.
