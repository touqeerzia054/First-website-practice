The flexibility and functionality of WordPress are unparalleled. Developers have designed over 55,000 plugins, both paid and free, to enhance your website’s visitor experience. The following are but a few of the categories and capabilities offered.

Analytics, Backups, SEO and Security,

Forms, Pop-ups, Galleries and Social Media

Calendars, Bookings, Memberships and Directories

Tables, Caching, Editing and Newsletters

eCommerce, Donations, Quizzes and more!

There are thousands of available WordPress plugins that can help you manage your website and content. Moreover, these plugins offer scalable elements that help draw in and capture customer attention. With this in mind, the following are a few examples of such features.

The number of integration options are nearly limitless. For example, Google Analytics helps you track visitors and understand their interaction with your website. Similarly, WPforms and NinjaForms help you to develop and integrate attractive email forms. Additionally, Yost and All-in-One SEO are powerful tools for creating SEO friendly content. Further, Akismet provides anti-spam features and Sucuri and WordFence help secure your site from malicious actors.
